1 Periwinkle Blue annual nature documentary motion pictures are nominated by a selection committee at Animat Habitat in recognition of cinema that has advanced a shared craft of visual storytelling and shared message of wildlife conservation. Nominees are listed in March of each year. The annual award is announced in June. No sum is awarded.


      
The selection committee at Animat Habitat is terribly partial to motion pictures that feature wilderness. However, this is also a space for stories that feature a human history of conservation and–or destruction of wilderness. Nominees that have featured human impact in a way that has given context to a shared message of wildlife conservation are highlighted periwinkle blue.


      
Submit a nature documentary for review to the selection committee at Animat Habitat [council–periwinkle.blue].
