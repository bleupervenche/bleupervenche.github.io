1 Periwinkle Blue nature documentaries are nominated by a selection committee at Animat Habitat in recognition of motion pictures that have advanced a shared craft of visual storytelling and shared message of wildlife conservation. Nominees are listed in March of each year. The annual award is announced in June. No sum is awarded.


        
* Annual Nature Documentary Award selection committee is terribly partial to films that feature wilderness. However, there is place to for films that tell a human history of conservation and–or destruction of wilderness. These nominees feature human impact in a way that has given context to a shared message of wildlife conservation.
